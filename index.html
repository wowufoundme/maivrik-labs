About Us
MAIVRIK stands for ML, AI, Vision & Robotics Incubation cum Knowledge centre. It has been set up at University Institute of Engineering & Technology (UIET) Panjab University, Chandigarh. It has been established to create industry-academic partnership by encouraging students, faculty and industrial members to push R&D in the aforementioned disciplines.
