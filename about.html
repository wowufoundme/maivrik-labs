Hello, we are MAIVRIK!
Building scalable, robust technologies from the latest human-like brains
MAIVRIK stands for Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Vision & Robotics Incubation cum Knowledge centre. It has been set up at University Institute of Engineering & Technology (UIET) Panjab University, Chandigarh. It has been established to create industry-academic partnership by encouraging students, faculty and industrial members to push R&D in the aforementioned disciplines.
Focusing on our vision...
We ensure that lab serves as a launchapd for new ideas/startups along with creating competitive solution for business enterprises in the domain of machine and deep learning.
Heading towards our objectives...
  • Promote industry-academia partnerships to foster consultancy and growth and create industry specific academic programmes
  • Encouraging students and faculty in conducting R&D in the fields of AI, Deep Learning, Vision and Robotics
  • Creating a center for academic and industrial innovation in research and new product development to attract global interest and investment
  • Promoting Entrepreneurship and Technoprenures amongst students and faculty members, in sync with Start-Up India Scheme
  • Mobility and international co—operation of students, scholars and faculty through international tie-ups
